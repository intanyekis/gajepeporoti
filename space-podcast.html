Over the Air — IoT, Connected Devices, & the Journey

The “Over the Air” podcast is a must-listen for anyone interested in the transformative impact of new technology in the aerospace industry and the ever-evolving world of space-based markets. Hosted by Dennis Muilenburg, former CEO of Boeing and current CEO of New Vista Acquisition Corp., this podcast delves into the exciting intersection of IoT, connected devices, and space technology.

Join Dennis as he explores the significant changes happening in the connected space, where connected devices and IoT are revolutionizing the aerospace industry. Discover the role of Special Purpose Acquisition Companies (SPACs) in driving innovation and investment in emerging space IoT technologies, and gain insights into the evolving ecosystem of space-based markets.

From cutting-edge advancements in aerospace engineering to the latest breakthroughs in satellite technology, “Over the Air” covers it all. Tune in to explore the impact of connected devices and IoT on space exploration, and get a glimpse of the future of space technology.

Topics Covered in “Over the Air” Podcast:
1. The Connected Space: Discover the latest developments in the connected space and how IoT is reshaping the aerospace industry.
2. SPACs and Space IoT: Explore the role of Special Purpose Acquisition Companies in driving innovation and investment in space-based IoT technologies.
3. Evolving Space-Based Markets: Gain insights into the dynamic landscape of space-based markets, including satellite communications, Earth observation, and more.
4. Future of Space Exploration: Get an exclusive look at the future of space technology and its potential to revolutionize space exploration.

Don’t miss out on the exciting discussions and expert insights featured on “Over the Air” – the podcast that brings you closer to the cutting-edge advancements in aerospace and space technology.

The Future of The Space Economy

The “Future of The Space Economy” podcast is a thought-provoking exploration of the opportunities and challenges in building a sustainable and successful space economy. Hosted by Carolyn Belle, Director of Advanced Systems for Astroscale, along with Andrew Maximov, CEO and Founder of Precious Payload, and Adrian Mangiuca, Vice President of Infrastructure at Voyager Space Holdings, this podcast brings together industry experts to discuss the latest trends and advancements in the space sector.

Emerging Space Technologies and Sustainable Space Economy

The podcast delves into the exciting world of emerging space technologies and their potential impact on the future of the space economy. From satellite constellations and space mining to advanced propulsion systems and innovative materials, the hosts and their guests provide valuable insights into the cutting-edge developments that are shaping the industry.

They also explore the concept of a sustainable space economy, discussing strategies to minimize space debris, promote responsible space exploration, and ensure the long-term viability of space-based activities. By examining the intersection of technology, policy, and environmental sustainability, the podcast offers a comprehensive perspective on creating a space economy that is both economically viable and environmentally conscious.

Space Entrepreneurship and Investment Opportunities

Another key focus of the podcast is space entrepreneurship. The hosts shed light on the thriving ecosystem of startups and innovators who are driving the commercialization of space. They discuss the challenges and opportunities for entrepreneurs looking to enter the space industry, offering valuable insights and advice for aspiring space entrepreneurs.

Moreover, the podcast highlights the investment opportunities in the space sector, examining the growing interest from venture capitalists and private equity firms. The hosts analyze the financial landscape of space entrepreneurship, providing valuable information for investors seeking to support groundbreaking space startups.

Houston We Have a Podcast

“Houston We Have a Podcast” is the official podcast of the Johnson Space Center in Houston, Texas. Each week, NASA takes listeners on a journey through human spaceflight, exploring topics related to Earth orbit, the Moon, Mars, and beyond. This podcast features in-depth conversations with astronauts, scientists, and engineers who make space exploration possible.

Stay informed about the latest updates in human spaceflight and gain insights from the experts at the Johnson Space Center. From discussing the challenges of living and working in space to the innovative technologies driving future missions, “Houston We Have a Podcast” offers a behind-the-scenes look into NASA’s endeavors to explore the unknown.

“Our podcast allows us to bring our audience closer to the amazing work being done at NASA’s Johnson Space Center.” – Gary Jordan, Public Affairs Officer at Johnson Space Center

Discover fascinating stories, groundbreaking research, and the incredible achievements of the dedicated individuals who contribute to space exploration. Whether you’re a space enthusiast or simply curious about the wonders of the universe, “Houston We Have a Podcast” is a must-listen.

Highlighted Topics:

  • Life aboard the International Space Station (ISS)
  • Exploration of the Moon and plans for future lunar missions
